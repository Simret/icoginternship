Journey at iCog Labs

Interview Process

My journey at iCog Labs began in May when I saw the call for interns and eagerly submitted my application along with my CV. After a few months of anticipation, I was thrilled to receive an invitation for an interview at the end of September. The interview went well, and I was officially offered the internship opportunity. My internship at iCog Labs officially started shortly thereafter, marking the beginning of an exciting and enriching chapter in my career.

Internship Process

As an intern at iCog Labs, there are several important steps and requirements to fulfill:

  1. Choose a mentor
  2. Take weekly training sessions
  3. Work on tasks with your mentor
  4. Complete online courses
  5. Write weekly reports
  6. Maintain 10 hours of office presence per week

I consider myself incredibly fortunate to have been paired with one of the best mentors at iCog Labs. After an insightful conversation, we carefully selected a series of online courses to help me build the necessary skills for the project. I began taking these courses one by one, diving into the learning process and expanding my knowledge step by step.
