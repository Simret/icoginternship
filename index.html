Journey at icog-labs

Interview process

My journey at iCog Labs began in May, when I saw the call for interns and eagerly submitted my application along with my CV.

After a few months of anticipation, I was thrilled to receive an invitation for an interview at the end of September.

The interview went well, and I was officially offered the internship opportunity.

My internship at iCog Labs officially started shortly thereafter, marking the beginning of an exciting and enriching chapter in my career.

Internship process

As an intern there are requirements to be fulfilled at icog.

  1. Choose a mentor
  2. Take weekly trainings
  3. work on tasks with mentor
  4. Take online courses
  5. Write weekly report
  6. Office presence for 10 hrs per week

I consider myself incredibly fortunate to have been paired with one of the best mentors at iCog Labs.

After an insightful conversation where we discussed my background, she took the time to introduce me to the exciting project she was working on.

Together, we carefully selected a series of online courses that would help me build the necessary skills for the project.

I began taking these courses one by one, diving into the learning process and expanding my knowledge base step by step.

courses I have taken

  • Supervised Machine Learning: Regression and Classification
  • Advanced Learning Algorithms
  • Python3 Fundamentals
  • Neo4j Fundamentals
  • Neo4j Cypher Fundamentals

Taskes I have done

  • Immproving prompt to summarizing graph
